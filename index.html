About Anti-Ragging Cell

The Anti-Ragging Cell of CMR Institute of Technology is committed to ensuring a safe, secure, and friendly environment for all students. Ragging in any form is strictly prohibited and punishable by law.

Objectives

  • To prevent ragging in the campus
  • To spread awareness about anti-ragging laws
  • To provide a platform for grievance redressal
  • To ensure student safety and dignity
